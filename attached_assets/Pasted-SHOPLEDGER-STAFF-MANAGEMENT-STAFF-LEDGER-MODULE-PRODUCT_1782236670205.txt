SHOPLEDGER - STAFF MANAGEMENT & STAFF LEDGER MODULE (PRODUCTION GRADE)

Objective

Design and implement a complete Staff Management, Staff Accounting, and Staff Ledger System.

This is NOT just an employee list.

The goal is to track every financial interaction involving staff members and provide complete money-trail visibility between:

Owner ↔ Staff
Staff ↔ Customers
Staff ↔ Vendors
Staff ↔ External Expenses

At any time, the owner must be able to determine:

* How much money is currently with each staff member
* How much salary is due
* How much salary has been paid
* How much advance has been given
* How much reimbursement is pending
* What money a staff member collected
* What money a staff member spent
* Complete financial history of a staff member

==================================================
MASTER STAFF ENTITY
===================

Use immutable:

staffId

as the only relational identifier.

Never use:

* name
* phone
* address

as relational keys.

Staff fields:

* staffId
* name
* phone
* address
* joiningDate
* monthlySalary
* salaryType
* status
* notes
* createdAt
* updatedAt

The existing Staff List in Settings becomes the master source.

Changes made from:

Settings
OR
Staff Module

must remain synchronized automatically.

==================================================
BALANCE CALCULATION
===================

Calculate:

Current Money With Staff

Formula:

Collections Received By Staff

* Advances Given To Staff

* Owner Transfers To Staff

- Expenses Paid By Staff

- Settlements Returned To Owner

- Salary Paid From Staff-Held Funds

Result:

Positive:
Money currently held by staff.

Negative:
Company owes money to staff.
