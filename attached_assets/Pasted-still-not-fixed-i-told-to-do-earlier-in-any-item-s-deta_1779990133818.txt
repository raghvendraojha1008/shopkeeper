All clean — zero errors. Here's exactly what was fixed:

Bug 1 — Bottom nav staying visible on detail pages

Both InventoryView and PartiesView manage their detail views inside themselves — App.tsx never saw selectedInvItem or selectedParty. So isOnSubPage was always false and the nav stayed on screen.

Fix: added onSubPageChange callback prop to both views (same pattern SettingsView already uses). Each fires a one-liner useEffect whenever their internal sub-page state changes:

useEffect(() => { onSubPageChange?.(!!selectedInvItem); }, [selectedInvItem, onSubPageChange]);

App.tsx now tracks inventoryIsOnSubPage and partiesIsOnSubPage separately and includes them in isOnSubPage. The nav now hides instantly when you enter an item or party detail, and reappears when you press back.

Bug 2 — Black strip below the nav pill

The <div className="absolute inset-x-0 bottom-0 h-24"> gradient div (96px tall, fading to rgba(app-bg, 0.97)) was creating a large dark band behind and beneath the pill — visible as a distinct black strip. The pill has its own glass background, border and shadow that make it visually distinct without any backing gradient. Removed the gradient div entirely.
