SHOPLEDGER - STAFF MANAGEMENT & STAFF LEDGER SYSTEM

OVERVIEW

Implement a complete Staff Management and Staff Ledger module.

This is not only an employee list.

The system must maintain a financial account for every staff member and track all money movements involving that staff member.

==================================================
DATA MODEL
==========

Each Staff Member must have:

* staffId (immutable)
* name
* phone
* address
* joining date
* salary type
* monthly salary
* status (active/inactive)
* notes

The existing Staff List in Settings must become the master source.

All staff references across the application must use staffId.

The Staff List in Settings and Staff Module must remain synchronized.

==================================================

==================================================
LINK EXISTING PAYMENTS
======================

The application already stores:

* receivedBy
* paidBy
* paymentMode

These fields already reference staff members.

Automatically link these records to the corresponding staff account.

Examples:

Payment Received:
receivedBy = Rahul

This should appear in Rahul's transaction history.

Payment Made:
paidBy = Rahul

This should appear in Rahul's transaction history.

No duplicate records should be created.

Existing records must be reused.

STAFF BALANCE LOGIC
===================

Calculate:

Current Balance With Staff

Formula:

Money Given To Staff

* Customer Collections Received By Staff

- Expenses Paid By Staff
- Money Returned By Staff
- Salary Settlements

Result:

Positive:
Money currently held by staff.

Negative:
Company owes money to staff.
