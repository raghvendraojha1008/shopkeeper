Balance With Staff =

  + SALARY_ADVANCE amounts      (cash advanced before salary day)
  - STAFF_EXPENSE amounts       (cash staff spent from their pool)
  - SETTLEMENT amounts          (cash returned to owner)
  - SALARY_PAYMENT amounts      (when paid from staff-held cash pool)
  + ADJUSTMENT (signed ±)
  - REIMBURSEMENT amounts       (net zero for cash flow — owner repays staff out-of-pocket)

A REIMBURSEMENT is a wash: staff paid from their own pocket (STAFF_EXPENSE that wasn't advance-funded) → owner repays. Net effect on "money with staff" = 0 after both sides are recorded.

  .filter(t => t.type === 'SALARY_PAYMENT' && t.salary_month === salaryMonth && !t.deleted)
